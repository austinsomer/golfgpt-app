## 6. Visual Identity

### Direction: Hybrid (Classic Core, Street Edge)

This is the sweet spot.

Not Augusta cosplay.  
Not loud streetwear.

**Minimal. Understated. Intentional.**

### Logo Concepts

- Circular crest with a minimal flagstick
- Clean serif wordmark: **THE LOOP**
- Secondary: simple `LOOP` block typography

**What to avoid:**
- No golf ball icons
- No gradients
- No cartoon marks
- No oversized logos
